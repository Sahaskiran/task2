⚙ Methodology
How the segmentation was performed
1
Data Collection
Gathered customer data: purchase history, recency, frequency, demographics, and category preferences.
2
RFM Feature Engineering
Computed Recency (days since last purchase), Frequency (purchase count), Monetary (total revenue) scores normalized 1–5.
3
K-Means Clustering (scikit-learn)
Applied K-Means with k=5 determined by Elbow Method. Features scaled with StandardScaler before fitting.
4
Segment Profiling
Analyzed cluster centroids to label segments: Champions, Loyal, At-Risk, New, Hibernating.
5
Actionable Insights
Generated targeted marketing strategies and KPIs for each segment to maximize LTV and reduce churn.
